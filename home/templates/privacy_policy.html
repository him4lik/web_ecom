Privacy Policy

At TestKart (the “Website”), your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our site.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit TestKart, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details like your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment details when you place an order (processed securely via third-party providers).
  • Browsing information such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • To send you updates about your order and promotional offers (if you’ve opted in).
  • To prevent fraudulent transactions and ensure security.

3. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, we may share information with:

  • Third-party payment processors to complete your transactions.
  • Logistics partners for delivery of products.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional communications.
  • Request information about how we handle your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
